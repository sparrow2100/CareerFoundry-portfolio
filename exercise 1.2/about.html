About

Hello! I'm Claire, a violinist-turned-web development student. After graduating with a degree in musicology and working as a violin/piano instructor, I decided it was time for a change. Luckily, I discovered coding and have been learning ever since! Web development allows me to apply the creativity and problem-solving skills I have cultivated throughout my music career in a new, exciting way.
