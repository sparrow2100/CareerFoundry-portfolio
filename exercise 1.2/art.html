Artist Portfolio

Overview

This is a portfolio website for an artist (Deb Shaw), that I created in consultation with her. It displays her bio, and information about different artworks.

View

Technologies:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Figma

Objective

Create a functional portfolio website to help an artist build her business. Make a design that allows for purchase functionality to be added once she builds up her art inventory.

Process

This is was a fun project, as I got to consult with an artist I know to find out what her vision was for her portfolio site, and make it come to life. I took notes on what she wanted the content to be, and suggested structures and colour themes. I also told her what content I would need from her, and got feedback on the design throughout the process.

Result

The artist is happy with her new portfolio, and excited to work on purchase and CMS features when her business is ready!
